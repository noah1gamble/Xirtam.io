My EUIV Projects

Currently, I have roughly 50+ planned mods for both Europa Universalis IV, Hearts of Iron IV, and Crusader Kings 2 as well as the upcoming Imperator: Rome. I love designing mods and adding onto an already great game. Europa in particular is one of my most valued games, and I have a lot planned for it the more I learn. This is a page for all my EU4 Major, Minor, and Submods that I have created. I will also link any translation mods or optional mods. Lots of good content here for EU4.
