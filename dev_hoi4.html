My HOI IV Projects

All of my mods for Hearts of Iron IV will go here as I have quite a lot planned and in progress. I have currently four major overhauls of the HOI4 vanilla game, with lots of content and tons of enjoyment to be had. My current favorite projects are Rerum Novarum; A World War 1 remake with an awesome storyline where the German Empire has won World War 1 and laid the fate of the future of Europe. I also have a very unique mod planned called Victorium which will be focused on a timeline where the Holy Roman Empire was not dissolved.
